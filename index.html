About Me
I am a Software Engineer on the Perception Team at Google Research.

I completed my PhD in 2018, working with Prof. Shmuel Peleg as part of the HUJI Computer Vision Lab. My main research interests are applications of computer vision using deep learning, particularly in the area of sight and sound-based video analysis.
